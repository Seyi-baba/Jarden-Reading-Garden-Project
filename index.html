Introduction

Jarding, an outdoor library and reading garden, offers a serene escape within the bustling Jos city, Nigeria. Nestled within the historic Jos Museum, this tranquil space provides an ideal setting for solitary reading, writing, brainstorming, and group study. Open to all, Jarding is a sanctuary for those seeking knowledge and inspiration. Here, the distractions of modern life are minimized, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in their chosen pursuits. Whether you're a bookworm, a writer, or a student, you're welcome to spend time at Jarding, either alone or with like-minded individuals. Jarding aims to cultivate a love of reading among people from all backgrounds. The library stocks a diverse collection of books, and visitors are also encouraged to bring their own. Book clubs, book launches, and poetry recitals are regularly held, providing opportunities for literary enthusiasts to connect and share their passions. Jarding extends its mission beyond traditional library services. By hosting discussions on topics such as the constitution, annual reports, and global issues, the space seeks to educate and empower the community. These discussions aim to foster critical thinking and civic engagement. As Nigeria's educational system faces significant challenges, Jarding strives to contribute to positive change. By providing a conducive learning environment, the library hopes to inspire young people and help bridge the gap between traditional education and lifelong learning. In a world filled with distractions, Jarding offers a much-needed respite. It is a place where minds can wander, ideas can flourish, and knowledge can be shared.
